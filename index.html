Memory Game

Welcome to our Memory Match Card Game

The game starts with all the cards face down
Select two cards to turn over and reveal the picture
If the two cards have different pictures, the cards will turn back face down
If the two cards have the same picture, the cards will remain face up
When all cards are face up, the game is won
Your score is calculated by the total number of click on the cards until the game is won
